How it works



The Dinomo uses state-of-the-art technology to quickly charge your mobile device. Along with its sleek, elegant design, the Dinomo pumps solar energy, heat energy, and kinetic energy straight to your phone. More than what you need? No problem. Our cases are fully customizable, so you only pay for what you need. That way, you, and your phone, are always in charge. Let your phone die no more; get a Dinomo today!


Solar panels

In a solar panel, crystalline silicon is packed between two conducting plates in which each silicon atom is bonded to four other silicon atoms. When light strikes the silicon atoms, electrons escape from the silicon atoms and travel through a wire to the Dinomo's energy storage unit, where energy is stored and ready to charge your phone.


Thermoelectric generator

Thermoelectric generators exploit temperature gradients in materials. If one end of a piece of metal is hot, and the other end is cold, the hotter end has more energy. As a result, the electrons near the hotter end will move toward the colder end, producing an electric potential across the piece of metal.


Motion-powered generator

A magnet moving through a coil produces a current. So we put three of these, one along each spatial axis, in the Dinomo, to generate electricity whenever your phone moves.
